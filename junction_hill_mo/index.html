Welcome to Junction Hill C-12 School District

The Junction Hill C-12 School District is located 4 miles East of West Plains at the corner of Highway 160 East and Highway 17 North. The school serves 215 students in grades Pre-K through 8th grade. Junction Hill C-12 School has been recognized with "Distinction of Performance" several times and performs in the top 25% of Missouri Schools for student achievement.
The Junction Hill C-12 School District has 11 students for every full-time equivalent teacher, with the MO state average being 14 students per full-time equivalent teacher
