Mission Statement

We're reinventing how your school shares content by making it easier for the content creator. Software should make your life easier and we are determined to help schools communicate more while making it more simple.

Single Platform for all your communication needs

You no longer have to update your website cms, facebook, twitter, mobile apps cms, send push notifications, sms, voice calls separately. Create content once and let thrillshare handle the rest. Imagine how much easier this will make your life.
